About

Born December 30th, 1984 to Gloria James. He was drafted by his hometown franchise, the Cleveland Cavaliers. After years of frustration with the lack of help he received on the roster, he left the Cavs and headed to the Miami Heat. He led the Heat to two championships befor returning to the Cavs. He won the hardest fought final series of all-time, coming back from a 3-1 lead. Unfortunately he then went up against the best team of all time for a few years before leaving to the Los Angeles Lakers. With the Lakers he led the disfunctional organization to its first championship and successful season in 10 years. The reason that Lebron is the GOAT is that he is truly positionless. He can guard and play any position. He isn't known for scoring, yet he has the all-time scoring record. He is constantly critized, but for 20 years now has played basketball at an elite level. He never had a "prime", his whole career has been his prime.
